The Color Wheel

This site is a visual guide to the nine colors of a simple color wheel, from warm reds and oranges to cool blues and purples. Each image below shows one color along with a short caption naming it.

The layout is fully responsive: on a phone you will see one color per row, while on a tablet or desktop the grid expands to show several colors side by side. Resize your browser window to see the layout change at each breakpoint.
